Linah’s Poultry Farm

Welcome, your trusted source for premium poultry products in Rwanda. Nestled in the heart of Rwanda, we have been passionately providing the finest chicken meat and eggs since 2018. Our farm is dedicated to sustainable practices that ensure both the health of our chickens and the satisfaction of our customers.

Quality of our product

At Linah’s Poultry Farm, we take pride in the quality of our chicken meat. Each bird is raised with care and processed locally, ensuring that you receive fresh, delicious, and nutrient-rich products every time. Our chickens are nurtured in a healthy and ethical environment, which reflects in the superior taste and quality of our meat.

Our farm-fresh eggs

Our farm-fresh eggs are another highlight of our offerings. Laid by happy and healthy hens, our eggs are packed with flavor and essential nutrients, making them perfect for any meal. We offer a variety of egg options, including organic and free-range, catering to different preferences and dietary needs.

Linah’s Poultry Farm

Linah’s Poultry Farm, founded by Akayesu Liliane in 2018, is dedicated to providing the highest quality chicken meat and eggs through sustainable farming practices. Our commitment to quality and community drives everything we do.
